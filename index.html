Ethics

Why Plagiarism in Code
is Unethical

Cutting corners in CP doesn't just hurt your integrity — it hurts your growth. You can't fake understanding in a live interview or on the job.

→
No Real Learning

Copied code doesn't build the mental models you need to think independently in interviews.

→
Interview Failure
